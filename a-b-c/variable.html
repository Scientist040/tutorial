Variable

Variable yana nufin ma’aji na data, wannan datar zata iya zama rubutu ne kamar “khalifa” ko lamba kamar 9999 ko kwanan wata. Ana hada wannan memory location din tare da bashi suna, a duk lokacin da aka hada shi computer tana tanadar da wani space na memorinta ta bashi sunan variable din, sannan duk lokacin da aka tambayi computer abinda yake cikin variable zata bayyana shi. Ana ambaton kalmomi guda biyu wajen kirkira da sanya abu a variable. Na farko declaring wanda yake nufin hada variable sai initializing shi kuma yana nufin sanya abu acikin variable. A Programming zamu iyayin declaring variable ba tare da munyi initializing din komai acikin sa ba ko mu bari daga baya muyi ko kuma mu yishi duk a lokaci.
Declaring Variable
Idan zamuyi declaring na variable zamu fadawa computer irin data da zamu ajiye kamar lamba ko rubutu sannan mu rubuta sunan variable kamar haka. 

int khalifa;
wannan shine declaring variable wato kirkirar variable ba tare da ansa komai a cikin sa ba. Anan abinda muke fadawa computer shine, muna so ta hada wani variable da zamu ajiye lamba aciki, alamar int itace take fadawa computer cewa wannan variable me suna khalifa na ajiyar lamba ne.
Note : Alamar semicolon ; tana zuwa a karshen ko wanne rubutu (statement) da akayi a programming, ana kiranta da statement terminator.
Initializing Variable
Idan muka so yin initializing a wannan variable zamu rubuta sunan variable tare da alamar equal (=) wanda anan muke ce mata assignment operator, sai mu biyo bayansa da abinda zamu saka aciki. 

khalifa = 8;
Declaring da Initializing Variable
Idan mukaso zamu iya yin su alokaci daya, zamu fadi irin data da zamu ajiye sai sunan variable sai mu biyo bayan sa da abinda zamu ajiye aciki. 

int khalifa = 999;
Khalifa

999
Atakaice anan abinda zai faru shine, computer zata kirkiri wani variable me suna khalifa sannan tasaka 999 aciki, to aduk lokacin da aka tambayi computer menene khalifa zatace 999.
