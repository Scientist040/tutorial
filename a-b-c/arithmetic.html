Arithmetic Operators

Wasu functions ne na math da ake amfani dasu domin gudanar da lissafi a tsakanin operand na farko dana biyu. Kamar 3+4 anan 3 da 4 operand ne ma'ana wadanda ake aiki ko lissafi akansu sannan alamar division ko tarawa itace operator. Gasu nan kamar haka:
Name Operator Misali
Division / 9/2
Addition + 9+2
Multiplication * 9*2
Modulus % 9%2
Exponentiation ** 9**2
Floor Division // 9//2
Modulus % Ana amfani da % modulus domin nemo remainder idan aka raba operand din farko da na biyu. 
 
 Int k = 5;
 Int a = 2;
 Int remainder = k % a;
 Print(remainder);
 //1
Exponentiation Exponentiation ** wannan shine power. Kamar ace lamba kaza power kaza, operand din farko ake daga power din ta akan na biyu. Misali: 

 Int k = 10;
 Int a = 7;
 Int exponential = k**a;
 Print(exponetial);
 //1000000
