
Auxiliary Verbs | Do
Kamar yadda muka fahimta a darussan baya Auxiliary Verbs ba kalmomi bane masu zaman kansu, kuma basa iya bada maana idan akayi amfani da su su kadai saide a hadasu da verb.
Auxiliary Verb na do yasaba da sauran, bari mu kalleshi a matsayin kalma ta aiki me zaman kanta (action verb).
Do yana nufin yin abu, kenan duk wata doka da take kan sauran kalmomin aiki zasu shafi wannan kalma. Misali:
Do - Doing - Did - Done
Akula da kalmar, na kara mata ing saboda present continuous tense ne.
Hakan yana nuna cewa kalmar tana aiki ta fuska biyu:
1) action verb : verb na aiki.
Da kuma
2) Auxiliary Verb: helping verb.
Idan muka kalleta a matsayin Auxiliary Verbs zamu iya amfani da ita wajen tambaya, misali:
Do you go to school? (Present Tense)
Did you go to school? (Past Tense)
Are you doing your homework? (Present Continuous Tense)
Saina maida auxiliary Verb na do yakoma baya tunda tambaya ce.
Idan kuma aikin yawuce za ayi amfani da past tense  dinta wato did
Did you go to school yesterday?
Kaje Makaranta jiya.
